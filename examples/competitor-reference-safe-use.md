# Example: Safe Competitor Reference

## Scenario

You want to use competitor product pages as structural references without copying protected assets.

## First Prompt

```text
我上传了真实产品图和竞品参考图。

产品图是我的真实产品，必须以它为准。
竞品图只参考画面风格、构图节奏和信息结构，不能照抄。
不要生成竞品里的产品、Logo、文字、包装、配色或品牌资产。

先做阶段一产品分析，再进入方向提案。
```

## Safe Reference Targets

- Page rhythm
- Text-safe area placement
- Module sequence
- Lighting direction
- General composition structure
- Visual pacing

## Unsafe Reference Targets

- Competitor product appearance
- Brand logo
- Exact packaging
- Exact copy
- Unique campaign visual
- Certification or trust badge
- Product-specific props tied to another brand

## Correction Prompt

If the output feels too close to the competitor:

```text
这版和竞品太接近。
请只保留信息结构，重写视觉方向。
首屏构图、背景材质、道具逻辑和情绪钩子都要明显不同。
真实产品仍然必须保持一致。
```

## QA Checklist

- The user's product remains the only SKU identity.
- No competitor product is copied.
- No competitor logo or packaging appears.
- No competitor text appears.
- The visual structure is transformed, not duplicated.
